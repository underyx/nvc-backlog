NVC Processing Timeframes

Case creation

Days elapsed after USCIS sends a case to NVC until the NVC creates a case for it in CEAC.

Document Review

Days elapsed until documents submitted on CEAC are reviewed by NVC.

Inquiries

Days elapsed until question and requests sent via NVC's public inquiry form are answered.

Data comes from the NVC Timeframes page. It's checked every four hours, and when new data is available, it gets committed to a JSON file on GitHub.
