NVC Processing Timeframes

All data comes from the NVC Timeframes page. There are many reports of cases that took shorter or longer than the numbers displayed there, but this is the best available official data.

Case creation

Time after USCIS sends a case to the NVC until the NVC creates a case for it in CEAC. Note that some time elapses after USCIS approval before they send the case to the NVC.

Document Review

Time until documents submitted on CEAC are reviewed by NVC. Note that there have been reports at the beginning of 2021 that immigrant visas are prioritized over non-immigrant ones, so if you have an immigrant visa application (such as the CR-1/IR-1) you can expect wait times a bit shorter than marked here.

Inquiries

Time until question and requests sent via NVC's public inquiry form are answered.

Data updates every four hours and is stored in a JSON file on GitHub.
